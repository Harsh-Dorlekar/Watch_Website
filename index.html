About Our Legacy

Established in 1870, WRISTWON has been a leading purveyor of exquisite vintage and luxury watches, catering to the discerning tastes of watch enthusiasts and collectors worldwide.

With a rich history spanning over 143 years, we have curated an extensive collection of timeless timepieces, ranging from iconic vintage models to contemporary luxury designs, each meticulously inspected and authenticated to meet the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.

Our commitment to preserving the heritage of horology is exemplified through our dedication to sourcing and showcasing some of the most sought-after and rare watches, ensuring that every piece in our inventory tells a unique story of elegance and precision.

At WRISTWON, we pride ourselves on providing unparalleled expertise and personalized service, catering to the needs of both seasoned collectors and newcomers to the world of luxury watches. Our team of passionate watch aficionados is dedicated to assisting you in finding the perfect timepiece that reflects your individual style and appreciation for timeless sophistication.

Explore our exclusive collection and embark on a journey through the captivating legacy of horological artistry with WRISTWON.
